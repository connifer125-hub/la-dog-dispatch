The System

Automation that moves
faster than the clock.

LA Dog Dispatch gives every at-risk shelter dog automated visibility the moment they're listed — connecting fosters, rescues, and the community before time runs out.

Why it matters

The old way leaves dogs behind

📋
No automation
Shelter listings go live manually — hours or days after a dog is euth-listed. The clock starts without anyone knowing.
🔇
No visibility
Dogs disappear into raw shelter data — no photo cards, no captions, no share buttons. The community can't rally around what it can't see.
⏳
Rescues hesitate
Without confirmed fosters, rescues can't say yes — even when they want to. Uncertainty costs dogs their lives.
📵
No alerts
There's no system to notify volunteers when a new dog is listed. You'd have to check Pet Harbor daily — and know to look.

How LA Dog Dispatch works

One trigger. Three networks. Activated instantly.

🚨 Trigger event
Dog added to euthanasia list
LA County shelter system · Scanned hourly
⚡ LA Dog Dispatch
Visibility page created automatically
Dog profile published · Network notified · Clock starts
📢
Social Sharers
Get the dog card and caption automatically — ready to post to IG Stories in one tap. Every share puts the dog in front of potential rescues and fosters.
✓ Visibility, instantly
🏠
Foster Volunteers
Sign up with their preferences. When a rescue needs a foster, they have a vetted list to reach out to — no cold starts.
✓ Ready when called
🐾
Rescue Partners
See available fosters and community visibility before deciding. Act faster with the full picture instead of starting from scratch.
✓ Pulls supported by the network
🐕
Dog gets a real chance
Rescue pulls · Foster receives · Community shares · Dog finds a home
