How LA Dog Dispatch Changes the Timeline

Today, many urgent dogs receive visibility only after:

  • A volunteer films them
  • A post circulates
  • Algorithms surface it
  • Pledges appear in comments

By then, days may have passed.

LA Dog Dispatch starts visibility immediately.

The moment a dog appears on the euth list:

  • A funding page is created
  • The dog is shared across the network
  • Donors can act right away
  • Rescues can see confirmed support

Every dog gets a chance. Not just the ones who happen to be filmed.

Your Impact

When you fund a specific dog:

  • Your per-dog donation is distributed directly to the rescue that commits to pull.
  • You receive confirmation when the dog is safe.
  • You know exactly who you helped.
