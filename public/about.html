The Broken Chain

The system wasn't failing for lack of heart. It was failing for lack of coordination.

Donors pledge in comment threads ‚Äî but rescues can't act on informal promises. Money that exists never reaches the dog.

Rescues hesitate without confirmed funding. They want to pull ‚Äî but saying yes without a foster or real dollars is a gamble they can't always take.

Fosters don't know where to plug in. There's no centralized place to say "I'm available" and get connected to a rescue that needs them.

The clock often starts too late. By the time a listing reaches the right people, days of the window are already gone.

We couldn't keep waiting for the government to modernize the system. So we built it.

The Solution

LA Dog Dispatch modernizes the entire chain.

The moment a dog is euth-listed, LA Dog Dispatch automatically creates a funding page ‚Äî activating donors immediately, alerting the foster network, and giving rescues the confirmed resources they need to say yes.

When the system moves faster, more dogs get a real chance. We hope you'll join this network ‚Äî or donate to help us save more dogs.
