2. About LA Dog Dispatch

LA Dog Dispatch is a coordination platform powered by Hot Girls Care, a Los Angeles-based 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. LA Dog Dispatch aggregates publicly available information about dogs on euthanasia lists at Los Angeles County animal shelters, connecting donors, fosters, rescue organizations, and volunteers to help urgent dogs find safety before their deadlines.

Hot Girls Care is the legal nonprofit entity responsible for donation management, fund oversight, and organizational compliance. LA Dog Dispatch is the operational coordination platform through which that mission is executed.

We are not a rescue organization, animal shelter, or veterinary service. We do not take physical custody of animals.

4. Donations

Donations made through the Platform are processed by GiveButter, a third-party payment processor. LA Dog Dispatch does not directly handle payment card information. All donations are subject to GiveButter's terms of service.

There are three ways to donate through LA Dog Dispatch:

  • Donate to a specific dog's fund — When you donate to a specific dog, we actively work to share and reshare that dog to maximize their visibility and rescue chances. If a dog is successfully rescued, we will communicate that outcome to donors. If a dog cannot be rescued before their deadline despite our best efforts, the funds are redirected to the next urgent dog on the list. Our mission is to save dogs — every dollar stays in service of that goal.
  • Donate to the LA Dog Dispatch fund — A general fund (available as one-time or recurring) that supports rescue coordination, platform operations, and urgent dogs as needs arise. This fund is managed by Hot Girls Care, the nonprofit behind LA Dog Dispatch.
  • Donate to Hot Girls Care directly — Hot Girls Care is the nonprofit organization that powers LA Dog Dispatch and runs community events including fashion shows and advocacy programming for dogs. Donations to Hot Girls Care can be made at the Hot Girls Care website.

Refund policy: We do not offer refunds on donations. All funds go directly toward dog rescue and care. If you have a question about a specific donation, contact us at hello@ladogdispatch.com.

Donor communication: We are committed to keeping donors informed. When a dog you funded is rescued, we will make every effort to notify you. When funds are redirected due to a dog not being rescued in time, we will communicate that as well.
